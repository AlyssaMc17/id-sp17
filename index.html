Coco Curry

Ingredents

  • 6 Cups Chicken Broth
  • 3 Tbs. Flower
  • 5 Tbs. Butter
  • 3 Tbs. Curry powder
  • Chilli powder (to taste)
  • 1 Tbs. Turmeric
  • 1 Tbs. Garmin Marsala
  • 2 Tsp. Cummin
  • 1 Tsp. Garlic
  • 1 Lb. Growned Beef
  • 2 Slices Mozzrella
  • 2 Sliced Carrots
  • 2 Sliced stocks of Celerry
  • 1 Dicced Pepper

Steps

  1. Bring the chicken broth to a boil in a meddium sized pot and add the carrots, celerry and dicced pepper.
  2. Seperate the growned beef into four eaqual parts, placed one slice of mossorella on two on them. Then take the pattys without mossorella and put it on top, being sure to cover all of the cheese.
  3. Melt the butter into a sauce pan,once meltel add the flower and wisk until brown.
  4. Once the flower is browned, lower the heat and wisk in the spices.
  5. After the spiced are thurraly mixed into the flower, add the rue to the pot. Mix in well.
  6. Put a lid on the pot and let simmer
  7. Take the two cheese filled burgers you had made and cook them in a pan, add a little oil if they begin to stick to the pan
  8. Once the burrgers are cooked to taste, put them on a covered plate to the side
  9. Finally plate the curry over rice with the burger on top and enjoy
